Omlette

Description

An omelette is a classic breakfast dish that is easy to make and customizable to your taste. This fluffy and filling dish is perfect for a hearty breakfast or a quick lunch. Here's a recipe for a basic cheese omelette that you can easily make at home:

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your favorite cheese)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs, milk or water, salt, and black pepper.
  2. In a non-stick skillet over medium heat, melt butter.
  3. Pour egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook undisturbed for 1-2 minutes, until the bottom starts to set.
  4. Use a spatula to lift the edges of the omelette and let the uncooked egg flow to the bottom of the skillet.
  5. When the top of the omelette is almost set, sprinkle shredded cheese on one half of the omelette.
  6. Using the spatula, fold the other half of the omelette over the cheese to create a half-moon shape.
  7. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the eggs are cooked through.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.

    9. Note: You can customize this omelette by adding fillings like diced vegetables, cooked bacon or ham, or herbs like chives or parsley. Simply add the fillings to the skillet after Step 3 and cook until they are heated through.
